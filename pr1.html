WHAT IS ASTRONOMY?

LETS EXPLORE THE SPACE!

Astronomy is the study of celestial objects and their phenomena. It studies evrything that exists in the universe and can be seen with naked eyes. Examples of celestial bodies include: the sun,moon, our planets,etc. Astronomy also includes various objects that are very far away from the earth. Such as galaxies, they are studied and observed with the help of a telescope. On this website you can always see new photos getting uploaded from NASA itself.
